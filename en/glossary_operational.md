## Introduction

This glossary establishes a shared conceptual foundation for all standardisation publications in the Belgian eHealth ecosystem, with the **CareSet** as its central concept.

Its purpose is to:

- ensure a shared understanding between business, domain, technical and decision-making stakeholders;
- clarify the precise role of a CareSet: what it is, how it is defined, how it is used and what deliverables it produces;
- clearly distinguish between:
  - the normative definition of a CareSet,
  - its implementation by the software, vaults and components of the ecosystem,
  - and the data instances actually exchanged and stored for a patient;
- make explicit the deliverables associated with the publication of a CareSet, beyond the FHIR specification alone;
- place these concepts within the existing governance framework, in connection with national and European initiatives.

The glossary therefore serves as:

- a cross-cutting reference document for all standardisation projects;
- an educational tool for understanding the structuring concepts;
- and a means of ensuring coherence between business needs, functional rules and technical specifications.
