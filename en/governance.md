## BeSafeShare – Role and evolution

BeSafeShare was originally a project focused on the CareSets stored in the vaults and published via the eHealth portal.

Over time, the BeSafeShare team:

- has acquired a cross-cutting role;
- contributes to several structuring projects:
  - Digital Referral Prescription,
  - VDIS,
  - Patient Summary,
  - Telemonitoring Prescription,
  - and other national or European initiatives.

This cross-cutting role now extends beyond the scope of any single project.

---

## Cross-cutting scope

The team historically associated with BeSafeShare retains a cross-cutting role, with the following main responsibilities:

- coordination and coherence of the business needs related to CareSets;
- functional alignment across projects;
- contribution to a global vision of the health data exchanged and stored.

Within this scope, projects such as the Belgian Patient Summary are handled as specific projects inside the cross-cutting perimeter.

---

## Governance and HL7 Belgium

In the governance framework:

- the initial definition of CareSets and of the business needs is led by the INAMI/RIZIV;
- the HL7 Belgium working groups, both business and technical, then contribute to:
  - the elaboration,
  - the validation,
  - and the publication of:
    - the technical implementation guides in the HL7 Build environment,
    - and FHIR resources and packages — FHIR CodeSystems, Supplements, Language Packs, ValueSets, NamingSystems — aligned with the FHIR ecosystem;
- the official publication of a CareSet within an Implementation Guide is the responsibility of the eHealth Platform;
- the official publication of ValueSets and RefSets, and the preparation of Belgian terminologies, is the responsibility of the FPS Public Health.
