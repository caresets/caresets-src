# Glossary

The BeSafeShare Glossary content is being developed, and this page is under construction.
{: .info-box .should}

The BeSafeShare Glossary is a collection of approved definitions that are used in the Belgian CareSets: These are generic definitions that apply to every use of a given term. For example "BusinessIdentifier" is defined as a Business  (not technical) identifier of a Careset, which can be a Patient, an AllergyIntolerance, a Vaccination, MedicationLine, etc.
The purpoe of a glossary is to ensure that information across caresets is semantically interoperable, i.e. meaning of the data elements are kept aligned to avoid interoperability issues.  

The Glossary is organised into the following sections:

[Clinical Glossary](glossary_clinical.html): Definitions of clinical concepts and terms used in the CareSets
[Operational Glossary](glossary_operational.html): Definitions of operational concepts and terms when designing and understanding the Belgian eHealth ecosystem
